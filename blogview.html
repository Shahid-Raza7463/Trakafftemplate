Exclusive API-PIN From Vertads CPA Affiliate Network

VertAds, subsidiary of Nova Media, a digital marketing company which manages all CPA campaigns from our in-house and valued partners on Meta, Google and other social sources. We provide high quality subscribers for mobile merchants worldwide with more than 30 Million conversions since established in 2019. Creativity, proficiency and credibility define VertAds and make it stand out in mobile tech solutions. And now, we are excited to announce that VertAds is offering PIN API for several geos, in addition to our regular services. So, what exactly is PIN API? In essence, it is a new way of hosting PIN Submit campaigns that we have developed internally at NOVA Media. Unlike regular PIN Submit campaigns, all requests are sent through an API. This feature is particularly useful for publishers who have their own apps or websites and integrate offers through API. Once the user enters their MSISDN (phone number) in the app or website, they can enter the PIN in the app/site manually or, more commonly, it will be pre-filled automatically. Once the app or site detects the PIN, it sends it to the TC PIN API, where it fetches the stored data using the tracker and checks if it matches. This process ensures that the user is subscribed to the campaign and can access the content or service that they have signed up for. The beauty of this feature is that it streamlines the process, making it more efficient and user-friendly. Rather than having to navigate through multiple steps and pages, users can complete the process with a few clicks. For publishers, this means a higher conversion rate, as users are more likely to complete the process if it is easy and straightforward. We believe that PIN API will be a game-changer for our clients who are looking to promote their campaigns more effectively. This new feature will save time and money, while also improving the user experience. With the latest update to the feature, the process has become even more seamless, allowing users to subscribe to campaigns quickly and easily. In conclusion, PIN API is a powerful new tool that we have developed to help our clients promote their campaigns more effectively. With its streamlined process, user-friendly interface, and the latest update to the feature, we are confident that this tool will be a game-changer for publishers looking to increase their conversion rates.
